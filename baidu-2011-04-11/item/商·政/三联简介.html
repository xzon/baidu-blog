生活·读书·新知三联书店是一家具有悠久历史和光荣传统的国家级综合出版社，前身是邹韬奋于1932年7月创办的生活书店、1936年成立的读书出版社和1935年成立的新知书店。1948年三家合并，成立生活·读书·新知三联书店。1951年与人民出版社合并。1986年1月恢复独立建制。

三联书店以出版社会人文科学的著译图书为主，兼及性质相近的实用书、工具书、电子书，包括用文学艺术形式表现文化、学术理念的图书。

三联书店在积极打造社科人文图书的核心竞争力的同时，努力推动期刊群的发展，现有《读书》、《三联生活周刊》、《竞争力》（三联财经）和《爱乐》等刊物。

三联书店始终以“竭诚为读者服务”为办社宗旨，以“人文精神、思想智慧”为坚守的文化精神，努力追求特色和品位，不懈追求创新与发展。

Shenghuo-Dushu-Xinzhi Joint Publishing Company is one of the national all-around presses which have a long history and glorious traditions. The former of SDX Joint Publishing Company, which were founded by Mr. Zou Taofen, are Life Bookstroe (Shenghuo), founded in July 1932, Reading Publishing House (Dushu), founded in 1936, and New Knowledge Bookstore (Xinzhi), founded in 1935. After the consolidation of the three is accomplished in 1948, Shenghuo-Dushu-Xinzhi Joint publishing Company became an independent press. In 1951, SDX Joint Publishing Company combined with People Publishing House. After 35 years, SDX Joint Publishing Company reverted independent.

The main publishing scope of SDX Joint Publishing Company covers humanities and social sciences. The publications we have published include works on philosophy, politics, history, economics and management. In recent years, the Company starts to set foot in the field of E-publishing and has already set up "multimedia publishing center".
